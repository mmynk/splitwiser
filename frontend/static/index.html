Split by item, not just total

Add individual items from the receipt and assign them to different people. Tax and tips distribute proportionally — fair splits every time. Perfect for splitting restaurant bills where everyone orders differently.

Track who owes what

Share bills with friends, track expenses across groups, and settle up easily. Whether you're splitting groceries, sharing trip expenses, or splitting rent — know exactly who owes what.

Free Splitwise alternative

Like Splitwise but free. Splitwiser is open source and free forever. No premium tier, no limits on friends or groups. Split with anyone using just a shareable link.

  • Share trip expenses with a group of friends
  • Split groceries by item between roommates
  • Track and settle shared rent or utilities
  • Split any bill with a shareable link — no account needed to view
